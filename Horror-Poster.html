Horror Poster - 2018

I created this poster in 2018 in my Graphic Design class. My teacher asked the class to come up with a scary movie idea and to create a poster of that movie using Adobe Illustrator. In my imaginary movie, there is a doctor who isn't who he says he is. He brings his patients into his own private building where eyes are set up around the place in jars. His only objective is to take your eyes in order to find the nicest eye to use for himself; which is why he has a different eye color for one of his eyes. Not only did I have to use Illustrator, but I also had to make a 3D model of the doctor in the position that I wanted since there were no reference images of anyone standing the way he is in the picture. I really enjoyed creating this poster; and I won second place in the competition that came with this as well.
