Stuffed Animal Photoshop Rework - 2019

This image was created in my Computer Information Systems class in 2019 using Photoshop. The assignment was to take an image that someone in the class wanted to change, and change it they way they asked using photoshop. This person asked for their stuffed animal to be placed in a different background than the original, and to get rid of their hand in the original image. I also added some cool effects that went with the background.
