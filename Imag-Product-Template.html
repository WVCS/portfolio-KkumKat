Imaginary Product Template - 2018

In my Graphic Design class, I joined with a partner to create an imaginary product. After coming up with the product, we had to create it's packaging. This is what me and my partners product's packaging would look like if it were actual made (not drawn to size). I created the background, did the layout/placed everything where it would look best, and I created the product image. My partner made most of the designs/images. We went with this type of packaging because we though the colors and layout went well with what we were trying to portray. The yellow and blue with the bubbles in the background show off our products cleaning ability; and the clean yet informative packaging gives all the information needed about out product. We had a competition of who would have the most amount of invested money in a product, and we won second place.
