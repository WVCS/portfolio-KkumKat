Typography Ad - 2018

This is my typographic ad for magazines created in my Graphic Design class using Photoshop CC. I decided to make a giant lotus that seems as though it's a big part of space comprised of the companies catchy slogans. The lotus is big because I wanted to show the main point of my company. The artist are our strong point and the beautiful art that they make are a big part of our company.
